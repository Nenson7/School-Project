Admission

Join Us

Thank you for your interest in Excellence School! Below, you will find the steps for the admission process.

Step 1: Application Form

Fill out the online application form or visit our office to collect a physical copy.

Step 2: Entrance Exam

Appear for the entrance examination scheduled at the school premises.

Step 3: Document Submission

Submit the required documents, including previous academic records and proof of identity.

Step 4: Admission Confirmation

Once selected, complete the fee payment process to secure your seat.

Eligibility Criteria

Students must meet the following criteria:

  • Minimum academic qualifications as per the grade applying for.
  • Successful completion of the entrance examination.
  • Required age as per grade level.
